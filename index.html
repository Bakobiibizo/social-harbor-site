The product

One private space for your people.

Harbor combines everyday communication with a local-first identity. No phone number, advertising profile, or social graph sale required.

Messages that stay yours

Conversation history is stored locally in SQLite and encrypted in transit between peers.

Voice and video calls

Start one-to-one or group WebRTC calls with clear participant and connection state.

Portable identity

Create an identity on your device and connect through shareable contact links instead of a central account.

Spaces beyond chat

Share updates and collaborate through local-first social and board views within the same desktop app.

How it works

Infrastructure helps peers find each other. It does not become the conversation.

Your deviceIdentity and local history Relay assistedDiscovery and connectivity Their deviceEncrypted peer connection

Harbor uses relay services when direct connections are unavailable. Message content and call media remain end-to-end encrypted; relay operators may still observe operational metadata such as IP addresses, timing, and traffic volume. The architecture and threat model are open for inspection.
